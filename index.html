Cleaning Checklist

1) Bedrooms

 Change ALL sheets and towels
 Place bed sheets in one laundry bag (Black); towels in its own laundry bag (Grey); blankets in its own laundry bag (Grey); Zip ALL zippers (Duvets)
 Dust ALL furniture, light fixtures, windows, baseboards, etc.
 Wipe off any stains, fingerprints, etc.
 Empty waste baskets & replace liner
 Sweep & vacuum floor
 Wet mop floor - O’cedar mop & floor cleaner
 Dry mop floor - Swiffer dry sweeper

2) Bathrooms

 Clean & sanitize sinks & faucets
 Clean & sanitize tubs and shower stalls
 Clean & polish mirrors, glass & fixtures
 Empty waste basket & replace liner bags
 Replace & refill towels & sundry items (shampoo, conditioner, bodywash, handwash, toilet paper, Q-tips, cotton balls, toothpaste, toothbrushes, etc.)
 Sweep, vacuum, wet mop then dry mop floors

3) Stairs/Entry/Hallway

 Sweep, vacuum, wet mop then dry mop floors
 Wipe off any dirt, dust, stains, etc. on walls and moldings, esp. baseboards

4) Dining Room

 Clean & sanitize tables & chairs
 Clean windows & curtains
 Dust all surfaces, esp. moldings
 Sweep, vacuum, wet mop then dry mop

5) Living Room

 Dust all surfaces
 Wipe & sanitize couches
 Ensure Guest Book is on the coffee table
 Ensure games are visible
 Replace throw pillow covers as necessary
 Replace throw blankets
 Check on 2x Full-size airbeds; change ALL sheets if used
 Clean windows and curtains
 Sweep, vacuum, wet mop then dry mop

6) Kitchen & Pantry

 Wash dirty dishes or run dishwasher
 Clean & sanitize countertops & sink
 Clean inside & outside of appliances
 Polish ALL stainless steel (faucet, appliances, cabinet handles etc.)
 Clean range vent filter
 Clean any dirt or stains on cabinet doors
 Empty waste baskets & recycle aluminium
 Organize dishes, pots, pans, supplies, etc.
 Replace & refill complementary items (coffee grounds & k-cups, creamer, sugar, tea, coffee filter, paper towel, display hand towel, etc.)
 Sweep, vacuum, wet mop then dry mop floors

7) Laundry/Utility Room

 Refill all laundry detergent & fabric softener
 Clean inside rim of washer
 Clean lint trap of dryer
 Sweep dust around washer/dryer

8) Garage

 Sweep floors, dust cobwebs & pickup bugs
 Ensure supply closets are locked
 Move garbage cans to the curb (pickup day – Tuesdays at DRGH)

9) Front & Backyard

 Pickup any trash
 Ensure everything around the house is tidy
 Ensure doors are locked before leaving

10) Final Walk-thru Inspection

 Check all areas for cleanliness
